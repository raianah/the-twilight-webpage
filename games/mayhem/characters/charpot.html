— THE EXPLOSIVE SPECIALIST —
CHARPOT

— DIFFICULTY —

EASY NEUTRAL HARD

— DESCRIPTION —

As a rejected chemist in Cybernetica, Charpot left the city and wander around until she was found in a toxic chemical facility. Creating hazardous materials and explosive bombs in a bottle. Charpot rose to fame after his creations, and was being hunted by the Cybernetics Corporation to shut her down.

— ABILITIES —

Poison Waste

Charpot throws a poison bomb that explodes upon impact, dealing damage to the enemy for two consecutive rounds and inflicts +1 Poison Mark.
