— THE PERPETUAL KILLER —
C'VAD

— DIFFICULTY —

EASY NEUTRAL HARD

— DESCRIPTION —

A former herder, now a psychopath and a lone murderer located around the mountains of Nix. C'vad gained his name after his previous victim named Kirshia Cavad, in which he mutilates her and scatters his organs everywhere. ... Some witnesses also saw him eating some of the organs and skin. C'vad always strikes alone during night to settlements, prioritizing women and children.

Aside from his enhanced skills like throwing large boulders, he can also manipulate snow storms into blizzards every time he is in danger against hunters. See More
