— THE RADIATED GLADIATOR —
DEMONA

— DIFFICULTY —

EASY NEUTRAL HARD

— DESCRIPTION —

A former warrior in her old, demolished town. These she called "intruders" have been raided her town, killed all elders, and kidnapped her. As she grew up, she became the gladiator to take avenge from what was lost. ... She ruthlessly slaughter every opponent she has. Outside her line of work, she became an assassin to murder the elites; the leaders who commanded the army to invade their land. Keeping her gem of life in her pouch, she will defend the last land and the last line of the tribe. See More

— ABILITIES —

Absorb

Demona's gem of life absorbs enemy damage, and uses it in her next attack as bonus damage.
